Fleeing Penguin

You are the last penguin of the north pole.
You need to flee extinction by globalwarming.
Use the mouse to move up and down and dodge the C O 2 monsters.
Collect the jetpack boosts (dw, it's green like planes) to move forward and the stars to increase your score.

This is a small light hearted game made to try out the p5js library, you can see the full source code on github.
This is actually a remake of one of my first 2d experiments i made in awt applets back in 2012! You can see a screenshot of the old version here.
